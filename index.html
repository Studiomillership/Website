Studio Millership is a London-based boutique architectural and interiors studio. We are experienced across residential and hospitality sectors and our work ranges from large complex commercial projects to individual homes.

We work with ambitious and inspiring clients who want to create beautiful spaces that tell a story and enrich all who experience them. By bridging the disciplines of architecture and interior design, we provide our clients with a unique design service that ensures our concepts are clear in expression, detailed in documentation, buildable through construction and uncompromised in use.

For us, each project must have a strong design focus, be firmly grounded in its locality and playful in its expression. Natural materials form the foundation of our palette, and we work hard to develop new and more sustainable approaches. We love to explore, delight in the detail, wonder at craft and strive to bring genuine and positive enrichment to peoples’ lives through experience of our spaces.
